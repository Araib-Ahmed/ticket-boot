Event Description

Dear esteemed members of InterNations Manila,

We are jubilant in inviting you to celebrate the 16th anniversary of InterNations this September 2023. And there could be no better place to mark this occasion than returning to one of our very favourite venues; Makati Shangri-La. Join us on September 16th from 6:30 pm to 10 pm, for a delightful event that will live long in the memory.

The well-situated luxury Makati Shangri-La, Manila offers exquisite rooms, suites and extensive dining choices with heartfelt and thoughtful service. And after a long period having closed in 2020, it's now finally open again. This time we will be at the 4th floor pool area, so come and join us for this celebration of sixteen years of InterNations.

Your entrance to the event includes the exchange of two drinks from the following selection.

DRINKS

  • Tempus Two Silver Series, Australia 2023, Pinot Gris
  • Tempus Two Silver Series, Australia 2021, Shiraz
  • SMB Pale Pilsen, Corona, Stella Artois
  • Bee's Knees, Pain Killer, Last Word
  • Botanist Gin, Belvedere Vodka, Glenmorangie 10 yrs
  • Soft drinks
  • Coffee

Don't miss this opportunity to experience the luxury of the Makati Shangri-La, Manila. For all Albatross members, you will receive the code in your mailbox.
We look forward to seeing you there!
