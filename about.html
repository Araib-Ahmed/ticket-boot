About Us

Ticketbooth.ph is an online portal for generating tickets for various events on behalf of event organizers.
We fully focus on events and happenings based in the Philippines.

As we all know, ticketing for event organizers is a key component to the success of their event as the risk components is the number of guaranteed attendees.
Selling tickets in advance is the best way to secure the attendance rate.Last and not least, we have partnered with some media platform to help you promote your event and can introduce you to more premium listing.
